### Container Units

|*Unit*|*Base*|
|:---|:---|
|`qw`|container width|
|`qh`|container height|
|`qi`|container inline-size|
|`qb`|container block-size|
|`qmin`|smaller of `qw`/`qh`|
|`qmax`|larger of `qw`/`qh`|

[Source: csswg comment](https://github.com/w3c/csswg-drafts/issues/5888#issuecomment-859884744)
